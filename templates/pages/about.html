About RealEstate

Why buy a house with RealEstate?

Quality, speed, an extensive scope of services and security – provided by the world’s largest real estate network. With RE you choose the right way no matter if you are looking to buy your first home, a new home for you and your family, a holiday residence or are looking for an investment. More than 1.8 million clients place their trust in us each year. Become one of them and let our RE professionals guide you through the process of buying a new home.
Advantages of using RealEstate

1. Enjoy an extensive selection of properties with the international RE network
With over 6,700 offices in nearly 100 countries all around the world, RE is the number one real estate company. Thanks to this extensive distribution network, the RE offer is huge. Our franchise system, with more than 100,000 licensed agents, is an active network in which all agents collaborate and share their properties. This is a clear advantage for clients as they receive a wide selection of suitable potential properties.
2. Save time with the professional guidance of RE agents
While RE guarantees a comprehensive range of properties, we do not overwhelm our clients with information. Instead, RE agents conduct an essential selection of those properties that meet the client’s needs. Services naturally include complete information provided in good time and on a regular basis, the preparation of contractual documentation for the parties involved, legal services, the notary deposit of money, arrangement of insurance and subsequent post-sales services (arranging removal services, etc.).
3. Benefit from the experience and excellence of RE
In more than 40 years, we at RE have perfected our real estate services and continue to pass on our consolidated pool of knowledge to our agents. All RE agents undergo various training courses on a regular basis and are continuously supported by the network in order to offer the highest quality of real estate services. This is how we ensure that our agents know best how to satisfy client needs – as THE local expert in their respective markets.
